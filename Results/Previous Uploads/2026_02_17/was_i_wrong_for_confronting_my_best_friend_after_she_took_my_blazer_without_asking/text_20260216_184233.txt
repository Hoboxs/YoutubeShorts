Was I wrong for confronting my best friend after she took my blazer without asking?

I recently hosted my best friend of over 10 years for a week. I’ve lived across the country for the past six years, so we don’t see each other as often, but we’ve always stayed close.

For context, I own a blazer with beaded tassels that she loves. It cost around $300. For the last two years, she’s repeatedly joked about me giving it to her, saying things like, “Don’t you just want to give it to me already? It’s more my style and you never wear it.” I’ve always laughed and said no, I love it.

While she was visiting, she asked again if she could “just have it.” I laughed and said no, but she was welcome to wear it out to dinner.

The morning she left, her flight was early. I was mostly asleep but heard her get up, make coffee, and head out. We hugged goodbye.

Two days later, I went to get dressed and had this weird gut feeling. I checked my closet, the blazer wasn’t there. I searched everywhere, including my laundry, because I genuinely didn’t believe she would take it.

I texted her asking if she’d seen it, thinking maybe she wore it and left it in my car.

She admitted she took it.

Her explanation was that she was “cold and running late for her Uber” and that it was “the only thing she was sure would fit.”

Here’s the thing: we’ve shared clothes for over a decade. Almost everything I own fits her, and she knows that. The blazer was in the back of my closet. She would have had to pass at least ten oversized sweaters and hoodies to grab it. The oversized hoodie she’d worn the night before was literally on the couch by the door.

Also, I’m generally very generous. During this visit alone, I gave her a few pieces from Reformation because I don’t wear them and she liked them.

It feels like because I said no to giving her the blazer, she decided to just take it anyway.

I replied with “wtf??” She repeated her excuses and then said she’d mail it back next week.

I told her that was ridiculous, called it weirdo behavior, said I was pissed, and told her to send it back and that I needed space.

Now I’m questioning whether I’m overreacting. Is it unreasonable to feel betrayed over this? And is it crazy to reconsider the friendship entirely, even if she mails the blazer back?
