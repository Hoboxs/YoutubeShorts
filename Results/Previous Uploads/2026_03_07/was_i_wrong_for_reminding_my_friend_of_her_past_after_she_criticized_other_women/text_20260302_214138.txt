Was I wrong for reminding my friend of her past after she criticized other women?

A friend of mine recently started seeing a new guy who’s pretty conservative. Since dating him, she’s adopted a lot of his views and has been posting frequently about “purity,” modesty, and traditional values.

The other day she shared a story on social media saying that anyone who sleeps with multiple people is a slut and should be ashamed of themselves.

That surprised me, because in the past she has told me about sleeping with more than one person. She has even teased me before for being a virgin.

I responded to her privately, not publicly, and told her I was confused by her post, given what she had shared with me before. I didn’t insult her or call her names. I just pointed out that it didn’t line up with what she had previously said about her own experiences.

She became very upset and claimed I was remembering wrong. Then she accused me of trying to shame her and throw her past in her face.

Now I’m questioning whether I crossed a line by bringing it up at all. I felt like she was being hypocritical and unfair, especially since she had mocked me in the past. But she clearly feels attacked.

Was I wrong for saying something?
