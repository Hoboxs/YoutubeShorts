Was I wrong for refusing to remove my engagement ring for someone else’s wedding?

My partner and I are supposed to attend the wedding of two of his long-time friends next month. We’ve all known each other for a few years, and while I’m not as close to them as he is, we’ve spent enough time together to be friendly.

They got engaged about a year and a half ago, and I got engaged a few months ago. My ring has a pale blue stone that can look darker in photos, which ended up becoming the issue.

We saw them in person for the first time since my engagement recently, and they asked to see the ring. The bride-to-be pointed out that the stone looked lighter in real life than it did in pictures, and that’s when the conversation took a turn. She reminded us that the bridal party would be wearing a very soft blue shade and mentioned that they had asked guests not to wear that color.

At first, it sounded like a light joke about my ring technically matching the color theme. But then it became clear they were serious. She told me directly that I wouldn’t be able to wear my engagement ring at the wedding because it was too similar to the bridal party’s color. She even said they had already asked someone else not to wear jewelry with similar-colored stones.

My partner and I were both caught off guard. I explained that I wasn’t planning to wear anything blue clothing-wise anyway, but that asking me to remove my engagement ring felt like a lot. It’s something I wear all the time and rarely take off.

They doubled down and said that wearing it would “cause drama” and might look like I was trying to draw attention, which honestly surprised me. I made it clear that I had no intention of taking attention away from them and didn’t think a small piece of jewelry would overshadow a wedding ceremony.

At one point, the conversation even escalated to them suggesting that our engagement timing somehow took attention away from their wedding, which felt unfair given the circumstances.

We ended the conversation without agreeing, and now it feels like there’s a real chance we might not be welcome at the wedding over this.

I understand wanting a certain aesthetic for a wedding, but being asked not to wear my engagement ring feels extreme. At the same time, I know this situation could affect my partner’s long-standing friendship.

I’m stuck wondering if I’m being unreasonable for refusing to take it off, or if this request has gone too far.
