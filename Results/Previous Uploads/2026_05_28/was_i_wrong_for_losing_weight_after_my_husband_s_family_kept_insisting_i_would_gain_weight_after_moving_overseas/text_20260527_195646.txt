Was I wrong for losing weight after my husband’s family kept insisting I would gain weight after moving overseas?

I’m 24 and moved to another country with my husband, who is 29, about a year ago. Before we moved, my mother-in-law and sister-in-law constantly made comments about how I was definitely going to gain weight once I got there.

It wasn’t just an occasional joke either. Almost every conversation included something like:

Everyone gains weight after moving here.

You should probably buy larger clothes before you come.

You’ll see, you won’t stay skinny for long.

At first I laughed it off and said I planned to stay active and keep eating normally, but they acted like weight gain was unavoidable.

To be fair, during the first few months after moving, I did gain some weight. Between stress, adjusting to a new country, eating out more often, and being out of my normal routine, I put on around 15 pounds. Eventually I started feeling uncomfortable physically and decided I wanted to make some changes for myself.

Over time I started cooking more at home, walking regularly, and paying more attention to my habits. In total, I ended up losing over 30 pounds.

I’ll admit part of my motivation was wanting to prove my in-laws wrong after hearing so many comments about my body. Their remarks genuinely got under my skin.

Now the situation has flipped. My mother-in-law keeps saying I’m “too skinny” and making comments that people will think my husband doesn’t feed me properly or that he’s somehow neglecting me. Meanwhile, my husband has been supportive the entire time and keeps telling me he only cares that I’m healthy and happy.

For context, I’m not underweight or doing anything extreme. I’m a healthy weight for my height and I eat normally.

Still, I’m starting to wonder whether I let their comments affect me too much emotionally. Part of me feels proud that I reached my goals, but another part wonders if making it into a point of pride against them was petty. Was I wrong for caring so much about proving them wrong?
