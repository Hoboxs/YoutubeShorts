Am I the jerk for secretly buying a house after my mother tried to take the one I paid for?

My husband and I entered into a rent-to-own agreement years ago involving a house that was owned by my mother’s then husband. The understanding was that once we fully paid the mortgage, property taxes, and upkeep, the house would become ours. Over time, we were the ones responsible for almost all costs tied to the home.

After my mother divorced, she needed somewhere to live, so we allowed her to move into the house with us. We continued paying all housing expenses, while she only covered her personal phone bill and credit cards.

When the mortgage was finally paid off, my mother told us she should keep the house instead. She said she had nothing in her name and that we were young enough to buy another home later. I did not want to turn it into a huge conflict, so I decided to look for a new house instead of arguing over it.

Every time I mentioned buying another home, she discouraged me. She told me the housing market was bad, interest rates were too high, and that it was not the right time to buy. Meanwhile, she continued traveling while we kept paying the ongoing household costs.

My husband and I eventually bought a new house in secret. We completed the purchase, received the keys, and only told her after everything was finalized and we already had a moving date.

When I told her, she immediately tried to lecture me about how irresponsible it was. I waited for her to finish and then told her we had already bought the house and were moving that weekend.

She became very angry and accused me of betraying her. After we moved out, she complained that we left her house in bad condition, even though we had spent days cleaning it thoroughly before leaving. She also refused to let us take some of our belongings from the garage, saying anything left behind now belonged to her.

She later told other family members a completely different version of events, saying she had generously let us live there cheaply and that we abandoned her financially while taking furniture that was not hers.

When she visited our new house, she criticized everything about it and then said she felt she deserved it.

Now she is struggling financially and keeps suggesting she should move onto my property in a trailer. I am not comfortable with that.

Am I the jerk for buying a house in secret and refusing to let my mother move onto my property?
