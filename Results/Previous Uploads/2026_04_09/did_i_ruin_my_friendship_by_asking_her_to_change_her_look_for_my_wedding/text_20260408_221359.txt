Did I ruin my friendship by asking her to change her look for my wedding?

I am getting married soon and I asked one of my closest friends, Marissa, to be one of my bridesmaids. We have known each other since we were teenagers, so having her beside me on such an important day meant a lot. I put together little gift boxes for all the bridesmaids, and inside I included a note explaining the look I had in mind for the wedding. I asked everyone to wear a green dress in any style, silver heels, and soft, natural makeup since I was hiring a makeup artist for the day.

Marissa has always had a very bold personal style. She wears dark clothing, dramatic eye makeup, and a very striking look that really stands out. I have always admired how confident she is with it, and I think she looks amazing. But for my wedding, I had pictured a softer, more uniform look for the bridal party.

A few hours after I gave her the box, she messaged me asking if that meant she was not allowed to wear her usual makeup. I explained that if she wanted to be a bridesmaid, I was hoping she would follow the same look as the others, but she was absolutely welcome to come as a guest and wear whatever made her feel like herself.

That did not go over well. She replied that if she could not be herself, then she did not want to be there at all. I tried to explain that I was not trying to change who she is, I just wanted everyone to match for one day. She said I was trying to turn my friends into part of a theme and that I cared more about how things looked than how people felt.

Since then, she has not spoken to me. I have heard from others that she is telling people she is not welcome at my wedding, which is not true at all. I feel like I tried to meet her halfway, but now I am wondering if asking her to adjust her look, even just for one day, was too much and ended up hurting our friendship more than I expected.
