My Boyfriend Thinks I’m Disgusting Because I Sit on Public Toilet Seats.

I’m 27 and apparently I just discovered that my bathroom habits are horrifying to other people.

Whenever I use a public restroom, if the toilet seat looks clean, I sit down normally to pee. If there’s a little water or urine on the seat, I wipe it off with toilet paper first and then sit down.

That has always seemed completely normal to me.

I’ve never thought twice about it because I am not sitting in filth. I only sit if the seat looks clean after checking it or wiping it down.

Recently my boyfriend of three years found this out because I casually mentioned sitting down to use a portable toilet at an outdoor event. He immediately looked horrified.

He started saying it was disgusting that I sit on public toilets and then come home and sleep with him afterward. He compared it to rubbing himself directly on toilet lids and claimed that anytime he kisses me intimately afterward he is basically exposing himself to “strangers’ bathroom germs.”

I honestly thought he was exaggerating, but then I asked a couple of friends about it and now I feel like I’m losing my mind because most of them agreed with him. Apparently a lot of people hover over public toilets instead of sitting.

The thing is, hovering sounds miserable to me. Also, if the seat is visibly clean, I genuinely do not understand the issue. It is not like I am licking the toilet seat. I am just sitting on it for thirty seconds like literally every person does at home.

My boyfriend now thinks it is weird that I never specifically told him this before, like I was somehow hiding it from him.

Meanwhile I’m sitting here wondering whether I accidentally became the grossest person alive without realizing it.
